Forest Block Studio

Greenrise District, Vismont

Studio 1 Bathroom Eco-Certified 100% Solar Energy

Tranquility and Oxygen

Forest Block Studio is located in the greenest district of Vismont. This studio apartment was designed to offer an authentic "shinrin-yoku" (forest bathing) experience right inside your home.

The walls are adorned with self-irrigating natural moss, and the furniture is crafted exclusively from reclaimed wood and local stone. The integrated Green-W-Olt technology ensures pure mountain water at every tap.
